<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/01-headers.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/01-headers-600.jpg" />

    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Populate Headers</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>Place the headers with the gap in the plastic matching the PCB legend</li>
        <li>Turn the PCBs over and set so the headers are sitting flush to PCB</li>
        <li>Solder the pins (don't trim)</li>
        <li>Do for both boards</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>


<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/02-jacks.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/02-jacks-600.jpg" />
        </a>
    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Populate Jacks</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>Place jacks in board, turn over</li>
        <li>Make sure jacks are sitting flush</li>
        <li>Solder the pins (don't trim)</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>


<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/03-switch.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/03-switch-600.jpg" />
        </a>
    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Populate Rotary Switch</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>Place the thicker pin in the larger hole</li>
        <li>Solder 1 pin, then make sure it's flush</li>
        <li>Then solder the rest of the pins</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>


<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/04-boards-cables.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/04-boards-cables-600.jpg" />
        </a>
    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Mount Boards and Connect Cables</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>RIGHT on bottom, LEFT on top</li>
        <li>Fasten with TRS jack screws</li>
        <li>Connect IDC Cables</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>


<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/05-chassis-top.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/05-chassis-top-600.jpg" />
        </a>
    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Mount Chassis Top</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>Put on top of chassis</li>
        <li>Put on SW1 nut and washer</li>
        <li>Fasten knob</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>


<div class="manual-step">
    <div class="step-image">
        <a href="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/06-case.jpg" target="_blank">
        <img src="%base_url%/assets/ssdiy/06-case-600.jpg" />
        </a>
    </div>
    <h2 class="step-header">Screw Case Together</h2>
<div class="step-description">
        <ul>
        <li>Use flat head screws for side</li>
        <li>Use pan head screws and rubber feet for bottom</li>
        </ul>
</div>
</div>
